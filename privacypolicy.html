Privacy Policy

Overview

At Artisan Essentials, we value the trust you place in us when you shop at our store. This Privacy Policy explains—clearly and honestly—how we collect your information, why we need it, and how we keep it safe. Our goal is simple: protect your data and give you a comfortable, secure shopping experience.

What We Collect

Depending on how you interact with our website, we may collect:

  • Your name and contact details, such as email or phone number.
  • Shipping and billing information when placing an order.
  • Order details and purchase history.
  • Technical data—like browser type, pages visited, and session time—to help us improve the site.

Why We Collect It

We collect this information to serve you better. For example:

  • To process and deliver your orders smoothly.
  • To contact you regarding your purchase or support requests.
  • To personalize and enhance your browsing and shopping experience.
  • To keep our website secure and running efficiently.

Your Data Is Safe With Us

We take data protection seriously. Your payment information is always processed through secure, trusted third-party payment gateways. We never store your card details on our servers.

Use of Cookies

Like most websites, we use cookies to remember your preferences, maintain your cart, and understand how visitors use our site. You can disable cookies in your browser settings anytime.

When We Share Information

We do not sell or trade your personal data. Some information may be shared only with:

  • Courier companies for delivering your order.
  • Payment processors to complete transactions securely.
  • Analytics tools that help us improve our store.

Your Choices & Rights

You can always:

  • Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Ask for corrections if something is inaccurate.
  • Request deletion of your information (where legally possible).
  • Opt out of promotional emails.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as our store grows and improves. Any changes will be posted here, and the updated version will take effect immediately.
