Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This page explains what we collect, why, and how we protect it.

Privacy
Last updated: November 26, 2025

Overview

Welcome to ARTISAN Essentials. We’re committed to protecting your privacy while delivering a clean, delightful stationery shopping and browsing experience. This document outlines the types of information we collect, how it’s used, who we share it with, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

Personal information you provide voluntarily — for example when you contact us, subscribe to emails, or place an order. This may include your name, email, shipping address, and payment details (payment processors handle card data).

Non-personal & Usage Data

Non-personal data we automatically collect: browser and device information, IP address (approximate), pages visited, and analytics data used to improve site performance.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide and maintain the website and services (order processing, contact responses).
  • Personalize content and product suggestions.
  • Improve the site through analytics and testing.
  • Prevent fraud and ensure secure checkout.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences, enable core functionality, and measure performance. You can control or opt out of cookies through your browser — note that disabling some cookies may affect site features.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers (payment processors, analytics, email delivery). These providers are contractually restricted from using your personal information for other purposes. We do not sell your personal data.

5. Data Security & Retention

We take technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption and access controls. We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

6. Your Rights

You can request:

  • Access to your stored personal information
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your personal data (subject to legal obligations)

To exercise your rights, contact us at: support@artisanessentials.com. We aim to respond within 30 days.

7. Children

Our services are not targeted to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, contact us to request deletion.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be posted with an updated “Last updated” date. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the revised policy.

Contact

If you have questions or want to exercise your rights, email: support@artisanessentials.com or visit our Contact page.
