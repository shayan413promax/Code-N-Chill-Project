Privacy Policy

At ARTISAN Essentials, we are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring a safe and enjoyable experience when browsing or shopping for stationery products through our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our website:

  • Personal details such as name, email, and phone number (only when you contact us).
  • Order-related information if you purchase any products.
  • Basic device and browser details for analytics and performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us provide a smooth, personalized experience. We use it for:

  • Responding to your contact requests.
  • Processing and delivering your orders.
  • Improving our products and website performance.
  • Providing customer support and updates.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. Your data is only shared with:

  • Trusted delivery or payment partners (only if you place an order).
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies to improve loading speed and user experience. You can disable cookies anytime through your browser settings.

5. Keeping Your Data Safe

We use modern website security practices to protect your data. Although no online platform is 100% attack-proof, we actively safeguard your information from misuse.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request:

  • Removal of your saved data.
  • Details of what information we store.
  • Corrections to inaccurate information.
